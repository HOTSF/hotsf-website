Transparency

Where Your Money Goes

The Hands of Time Scholars Foundation is committed to transparent stewardship of your contributions. We are building a lean, volunteer-driven organization where the maximum possible percentage of every dollar goes directly toward student scholarships.

As we formalize our operations, we will publish annual reports detailing how funds are allocated across scholarship awards, program administration, and outreach. Our goal is to ensure your generosity has the greatest possible impact on the next generation of watchmakers.

Tax-Deductible Giving

The Hands of Time Scholars Foundation has applied for 501(c)(3) tax-exempt status. Once approved, donations will be tax-deductible to the fullest extent of the law, retroactive to our date of incorporation. You will receive a written acknowledgment for your records. Please consult your tax advisor for details on deducting charitable contributions.
