The Proud Supporter Program

Elevate Your Brand With Purpose

The Hands of Time Partnership Program welcomes watch brands, authorized dealers, luxury retailers, and philanthropic organizations. As a partner, you'll display the distinguished Proud Supporter crest — signaling to your customers and community that your organization is invested in the future of the trade.

In an industry built on heritage and craft, supporting watchmaking education is the ultimate expression of commitment to the art form.

Getting Started

How It Works

Choose Your Tier

Select the partnership level that aligns with your business goals and budget. Every tier makes a meaningful impact.

Complete Your Partnership

We'll send you a partnership agreement and invoice. Once processed, you're officially a Proud Supporter.

Receive Your Assets

Get your digital crest badge, certificate, and any tier-specific marketing materials within 5 business days.

Showcase Your Support

Display the Proud Supporter crest in your store, on your website, and across social media. We'll amplify your commitment through our channels.

The Opportunity

Why Partner With Us?

ESG & Social Impact

Today's consumers expect businesses to give back. A partnership with the Hands of Time Scholars Foundation demonstrates your commitment to social responsibility and industry sustainability.

Customer Differentiation

Stand out from competitors by aligning your brand with a prestigious cause. Watch enthusiasts value businesses that invest in the craft's future.

Luxury Brand Cachet

The Proud Supporter crest signals taste, commitment, and values — attributes that resonate with discerning luxury consumers.
