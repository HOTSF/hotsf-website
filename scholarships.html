Scholarship Program

Scholarships for Tomorrow's Watchmakers

Providing financial support to talented students pursuing careers in horology at accredited programs nationwide.

Program Overview

Investing in the Next Generation

The Hands of Time Scholars Foundation awards scholarships of $2,500 to $5,000 per recipient to students enrolled in — or accepted to — accredited watchmaking programs in the United States. Funds are disbursed directly to the educational institution or to the student, depending on the program structure.

Our scholarships are designed to ease the financial burden of watchmaking education, which requires significant investment in tuition, tools, and specialized materials. We believe talent and passion — not financial circumstances — should determine who gets to pursue this rewarding craft.

Applications Open Late 2026

The first Hands of Time scholarships will be awarded for the 2027 scholarship cycle. Applications are expected to open late 2026. Sign up for our newsletter on the homepage to be notified when applications go live.

Who Can Apply

Eligibility Requirements

  • US-based — applicants must be located in the United States
  • Enrolled or accepted at an accredited watchmaking program
  • Demonstrate financial need or exceptional commitment to the craft
  • Open to students of all ages and backgrounds

How to Apply

Application Requirements

Personal Statement

Tell us about your passion for horology, your goals, and why you're pursuing a career in watchmaking.

Resume or CV

Provide your educational background and any relevant experience, whether in horology or other fields.

One Letter of Recommendation

A letter from a teacher, mentor, employer, or someone familiar with your character and commitment.

Proof of Enrollment

Acceptance letter or enrollment confirmation from an accredited watchmaking program.

Optional: 60-Second Video

A short video introducing yourself and sharing what drives your passion for watchmaking. This is optional but encouraged.
